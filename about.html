About Even Flow Yoga

Even Flow Yoga Studio was started with the sole intention of

creating the best experience for all your wellbeing needs.

Our studios provide Yoga and meditation to help you return to your true self. We want all our teachers, students and staff to feel within their heart,

body and mind that they are at home when they step into our studios. Our Yoga honours both the history of yoga as well as its ever-changing

landscape. Only the best teachers with real personal practices and commitment to their professional development are employed by our studio.


We offer a wide range of authentic styles so that you can find the practice that suits you. Everybody, no

matter what age, size, gender or fitness level will find something that they can enjoy and thrive in.
